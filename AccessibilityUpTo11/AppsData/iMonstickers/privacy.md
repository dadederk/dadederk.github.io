# Privacy Policy for iMonstickers

**Effective Date:** January 4, 2026

**iMonstickers** is a Messages sticker pack developed by Daniel Devesa Derksen-Staats. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using the app.

## What information do we collect?

iMonstickers does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data**.

As a Messages sticker pack, iMonstickers operates entirely within the Messages app framework and has no capability to collect or transmit data. All stickers are stored locally on your device.

## Third-party services

iMonstickers does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
