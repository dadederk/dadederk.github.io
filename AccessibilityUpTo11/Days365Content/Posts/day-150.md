Today I have a question for you: which books have helped you level up your accessibility game?

I'll start: [*Developing Inclusive Mobile Apps*](https://amzn.to/2KSDmaz) by Rob Whitaker ([@RobW](https://iosdev.space/@RobW)). It is not just iOS—you also learn about Android, inclusive design, and WCAG.
